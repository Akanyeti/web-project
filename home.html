Research

My research draws from the fields of biology, engineering and computer science. I investigate neural, biomechanical and hydrodynamic mechanisms of how aqutic animals interact with their surroundings. From these observations, I develop new bio-inspired technologies for underwater robotics applications. My research has led to 20 journal articles published to date in leading robotics and biological journals including Nature Communications, PNAS and Cell Reports.
